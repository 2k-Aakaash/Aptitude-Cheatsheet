Analytical Reasoning

Enhance your analytical skills with various problem types.

Seating Arrangement

Understand and solve problems involving seating arrangements.

Example Problem:

Five friends are sitting in a row. A is to the left of B, but right of C. D is to the right of B but left of E. Who is sitting in the middle?

Solution:

The order is C, A, B, D, E. B is in the middle.

Blood Relations

Analyze and solve problems related to family and blood relations.

Example Problem:

A is the father of B. B is the mother of C. How is A related to C?

Solution:

A is the grandfather of C.

Logical Puzzles

Solve various logical puzzles to test your reasoning abilities.

Pattern Recognition

Identify patterns in sequences and series.

Example Problem:

What comes next in the sequence: 2, 6, 12, 20, ...?

Solution:

The sequence increases by 4, 6, 8, 10, so the next number is 30.

Syllogisms

Understand and solve syllogism problems.

Example Problem:

Statements: All cats are animals. Some animals are birds. Conclusions: 1. Some cats are birds. 2. No cat is a bird.

Solution:

Neither conclusion follows.
