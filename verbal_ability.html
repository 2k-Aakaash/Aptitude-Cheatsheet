Verbal Ability Cheatsheet

Enhance your verbal skills with these key concepts

Reading Comprehension

Improve your reading comprehension skills with practice.

Understanding Passages

Learn to read and understand passages quickly and accurately.

Example Problem:

Read the following passage and answer the questions below:

“The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. This pangram contains every letter in the English alphabet. Such sentences are often used for typing practice and font display purposes.”

Question: What is the primary purpose of the given sentence?

Solution:

The primary purpose is to contain every letter in the English alphabet.

Critical Reasoning

Develop your ability to analyze arguments and identify logical flaws.

Example Problem:

Identify the assumption in the argument: “If it rains, the match will be canceled. Therefore, if the match is canceled, it must have rained.”

Solution:

The assumption is that the match can only be canceled due to rain.

Grammar

Master the rules of grammar to improve your writing and speaking skills.

Sentence Correction

Identify and correct grammatical errors in sentences.

Example Problem:

Find the error in the sentence: “Each of the boys have their own book.”

Solution:

The correct sentence is: “Each of the boys has his own book.”

Fill in the Blanks

Choose the correct word or phrase to complete a sentence.

Example Problem:

Fill in the blank: “She has a _____ for painting.”

Solution:

The correct word is: “talent.”
